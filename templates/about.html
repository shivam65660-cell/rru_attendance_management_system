About This Software

This software is a web-based attendance management system developed for Rashtriya Raksha University (RRU) Gandhinagar gujarat and all campus. Its primary goal is to provide a seamless and efficient way for students and teachers to manage and track attendance records.

Key Features

  • Secure Authentication: The system provides a secure login and registration process for both students and teachers, ensuring that all data is kept private and accessible only to authorized users.
  • Attendance Tracking: Teachers can easily mark the attendance of their students, while students can view their attendance history at any time.
  • Data Management: The system stores user and attendance data, making it easy to generate and view reports as needed.
